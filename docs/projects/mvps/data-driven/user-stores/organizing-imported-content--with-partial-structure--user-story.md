# organizing-imported-content--with-partial-structure--user-story

## As a …

**Context · Role · Situation**

> Mary has some loosely organized content for a website she is making. It is in P.A.R.A. subdirectories. She knows that she wants to have some content moved from `resources/terrible-directory-name` to `resources/great-name`, but other items need to go somewhere else and she is not yet sure where. She includes `resources/great-name` in her definition but leaves the rest undecided.

---

## I want to …

**Goal · Action · Intent**

> She would like to review the unorganized documents, and thinks that adding metadata may help her revisit and reassess the content over time.
>
> She may want to start by defining document titles.

---

## So that …

**Motivation · Value · Expected Outcome**

> So that she can organize the content thoughtfully without forcing premature decisions.

---

## Notes (Optional)

This story intentionally reflects **partial certainty**.  
Some destinations are known, others are explicitly deferred.

This is not a failure state—it is the starting condition.
